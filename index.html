Why KlipPal?

Your Copy History

Everything you copy is saved automatically. Go back and find that thing you copied yesterday, or last week.

Find It Instantly

Just start typing to search through everything you've copied. Even if you don't remember the exact words.

Simple to Use

Lives quietly in your menu bar. Press a shortcut to find what you need, then get back to work.

100% Private

Your clipboard history never leaves your Mac. No accounts, no cloud, no data collection. What you copy stays with you.

Pin Important Items

Keep your most-used clipboard items safe. Pinned items never get deleted, even when you reach your history limit.

Secure by Design

No servers, no tracking, no analytics. We can't see your data because it never touches our systems.
